The Human Body Game

Dive into the exciting world inside you with The Human Body Game! Explore different systems of your body, from bones to organs, and see how they all work together in this interactive simulation.

Use interactive simulations to label parts, learn their functions, identify bones on a skeleton and much more. It's a fun way to discover the amazing secrets of the human body. Are you ready to start your journey inside?

Science, Biology
7-11 Years
Game

The Human Body Game offers an engaging educational experience for students aged 7-11 to learn about the structure and functions of the human body.

Through interactive simulations, drag-and-drop gameplay, and quiz-based learning, students will explore various bodily systems, diagram, and deepen their understanding of biology. This resource effectively demonstrates how the body works, emphasising how these specific systems process air and food, making it easier for students to grasp complex concepts in a memorable way.
